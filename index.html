Ethan Manilow is a PhD candidate studying Computer Science working under Bryan Pardo in the Interactive Audio Lab at Northwestern University. He has interned at MERL on the Speech and Audio Team, and at Google Brain on the Magenta Team. He lives in Chicago, where he spends his free time playing his guitar and smiling at dogs he passes on the sidewalk.
